Who We Are

Two estates, one supply chain.

Bespat Agri-Trade and Farms is a commercially driven agribusiness positioned at the intersection of large-scale food production, industrial raw material supply, and regional agricultural trade. We operate two fully functional farming estates in Mchinji and Nkhotakota.

Our dual-farm structure allows us to diversify production across grains, legumes, and sugarcane, ensuring stability of output and resilience against seasonal and market fluctuations — supplying both domestic food security needs and major industrial processors.
