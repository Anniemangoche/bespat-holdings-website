Who We Are

A diversified Malawian group, built on discipline.

Bespat Holdings Limited began as a single construction company and has grown into a six-division group spanning building & civil works, human capital outsourcing, freight logistics, petroleum retail, FMCG distribution, and commercial agriculture.

What ties the Group together isn't a shared product — it's a shared discipline. Every division is run on the same principles: full statutory compliance, transparent financial controls, and a healthy cash position that lets us pay our people and meet our obligations without waiting on client payment cycles.

Across construction, fuel retail, distribution, logistics and agriculture, the Group now supports a combined workforce of 290+ people and contributes meaningfully to Malawi's Vision 2063 ambitions for an industrialised, self-reliant economy.
