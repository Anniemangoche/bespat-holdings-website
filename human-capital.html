Who We Are

More than a recruiter — a workforce partner.

We are more than a human resources firm; we are a trusted partner in workforce transformation. We believe the right people are the most powerful drivers of success, and we manage that relationship with integrity, insight, and impact.

Our mission is to deliver innovative, professional, and impact-driven HR solutions that advance skills development and strengthen organisational performance across Malawi's public and private sectors — accelerating Malawi Vision 2063 by nurturing a competent, industrialising workforce.

What We Do

End-to-end human capital services.

Seven service lines covering the full employee lifecycle — from talent acquisition to workforce outsourcing.

01

Strategic Talent Acquisition

Competency-based assessments and behavioural interviewing to find candidates who fit your culture, not just your job spec.

02

Structured Onboarding & Orientation

New hires immersed in workplace culture, values, and performance expectations within the first week.

03

Performance Management Systems

KPI-aligned frameworks with bi-annual reviews, continuous feedback, and Performance Improvement Plans where needed.

04

Competency-Based Training

Client-driven learning interventions including soft-skills enhancement and continuous professional development.

05

Employee Engagement & Welfare

Recognition, communication, and work-life balance initiatives that lower absenteeism and build loyalty.

06

HR Compliance & Industrial Relations

Full alignment with local labour law, union negotiation support, and grievance handling.

07

Workforce Planning & Outsourcing

Scalable outsourcing models managing recruitment, contracts, payroll, and performance on your behalf — backed by our own healthy cashflow, so your workforce gets paid on time even when invoicing cycles run long.
