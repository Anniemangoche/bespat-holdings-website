Who We Are

Building Malawi's infrastructure, project by project.

BESPAT Construction Company was established and registered as a general works contractor in both Building and Civil Engineering with the National Construction Industry Council. Over the years, our directors and team have accumulated deep technical know-how in building and civil solutions, project management, and related engineering.

Our objective is to provide our clients with an "I am assured" experience when chosen to execute projects. Our emphasis on clear communication and follow-through procedures ensures that client objectives are top priority in the planning and execution of all our processes.

Our vision is to be a highly respected and trusted building contractor in Malawi and beyond — a One Stop Centre covering the entire project lifecycle from design and engineering through to project management and execution.

What We Do

Five service lines, one site team.

From site establishment to final finishes — we handle every phase of construction with precision and safety.

01

Site Establishment & Secure Boundaries

Perimeter hoarding, safe access routes, site offices and welfare facilities set up from day one of every project.

02

Structural & Superstructure Works

Reinforced concrete frames, structural steelwork, masonry walls, and roofing systems built to design specification and load requirements.

03

Roads, Pavements & Drainage

Durable access roads, parking areas, and pedestrian pavements integrated with drainage channels, culverts, and manholes.

04

Building Services & Utility Installation

Electrical wiring, water supply, sewage networks, and fire safety systems installed to Malawi OSHA and building code standards.

05

Finishing Works, Fit-Out & Refurbishment

Plastering, painting, tiling, ceilings, and interior fit-outs that respect existing structures while bringing them up to modern standards with minimal disruption to occupants.
